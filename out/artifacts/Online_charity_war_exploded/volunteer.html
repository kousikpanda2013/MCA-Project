VOLUNTEER REQUIREMENTS

In order to volunteer at our organization, you just need to fill out the Volunteer Application, attend an orientation, follow the rules for safety, and bring a great attitude! It’s that simple!
Once you have done so, we can schedule you for volunteering.

Application

Complete the volunteer application online and fill your details for being a volunteer and help us.

Orientation

After submitting application online Successfully.Orientation is required for individuals prior to getting placed on our volunteer schedule. However, if you are looking to schedule a group or you cannot attend a scheduled orientation. If any problem occurred please contact volunteer services at-8001993699.

Opportunities

During the orientation, you will be presented with our current volunteer opportunities.
Stipend:5000(It will depend upon your work performance and donation).

Dress

We Will Provide a particular dress for our volunteer. All Dress are comfortable, but please remember no sandals, heels or open-toed shoes in the warehouse, and wear layers. In the winter, it’s cold in the warehouse and in the summer it can get very warm.

Age Issue

A volunteer age must be between 18 to 25 years.A volunteer must be a citizen of India.

Documents

1. Photocopy of aadhaar card along with original aadhaar card.
2.Any age prove certificate along with original.
3.Two copy recent passport size photograph.
